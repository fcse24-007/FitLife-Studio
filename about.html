Get To Know Us

At FitLife Studio, we don’t just count reps — we build habits, confidence, and a community that lifts each other higher. Whether you're chasing gains, goals, or good vibes, we’ve got the space, the support, and the sweat to get you there. Come for the workout. Stay for the transformation.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, FitLife Studio was born from a passion for helping people transform their lives through fitness.

What started as a small personal training studio has grown into Gaborone's premier fitness destination. Our journey has been driven by the success stories of our members and the dedication of our expert trainers.

Today, we continue to evolve and expand our offerings while maintaining our core commitment to personalized attention and exceptional service.
